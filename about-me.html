What I Solve

I’m a UX researcher and designer with a focus on how people make decisions online.

My work centers on one question: “What's stopping users from moving forward?”

Through research, testing, and iteration, I identify where trust breaks down, where information gets lost, and where users hesitate and I design solutions that remove those barriers.

In my recent work with a local catering business, I helped restructure their website experience to:

  • Make pricing easier to find
  • Clarify service options
  • Reduce the need for back-and-forth communication

The result: faster decisions, stronger trust, and a smoother path to booking.
